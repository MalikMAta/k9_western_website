The Bay Areas Dog Trainer

Dog training starts with the owner. Whether it's obedience or behavior modification, we believe it's important to have a happier dog and we can give you the training you need that will last a lifetime

Who we are

At western k9 we are dedicated to helping every dog owner who come to us for help. Our mission is to create a stronger and lasting bond between dog and handler so your dog can live a happier life.

How we do it

No two dogs are the same so we don't train every dog the same. Thats why we tailor your training to your specific needs using our proven and balanced system.
